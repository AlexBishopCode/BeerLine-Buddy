BeerLine Buddy

A step-by-step tutorial on how to clean a cask beer line


STEP 1

Fill two buckets with clean water. Pour 100ml of line cleaner into one of the buckets.

STEP 2

Secure the beer line into the base of the bucket of pure water and pull water through the line until until the water runs clear.

STEP 3

Transfer the line into the bucket containing the line cleaner.

STEP 4

Pull the line cleaner through the line and test the litmus paper to check the paper turns pink.

STEP 5

Click start to activate timer 1

STEP 6

Click start to activate timer 2

STEP 7

Click start to activate timer 3

STEP 8

Remove the line from the bucket and place into a bucket of clean water.

STEP 9

Pull the clean water through the line.

STEP 10

Using litmus paper, pull the water through the line until a litmus paper test returns clear.

STEP 11

Remove the beer line from the bucket


The line is now clean.
